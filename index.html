How to Play Blackjack:

The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

- Aces are worth 1 or 11 (whichever proves beneficial), face cards are worth 10.
- You and the dealer each get 2 cards. One of the dealer's cards is hidden.
- Press "Hit" to draw another card.
- Press "Stand" to end your turn.
- The dealer must draw until they have 17 or more.
- If your total is higher than the dealer’s (without going over 21), you win!
